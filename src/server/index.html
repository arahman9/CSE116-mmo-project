Welcome to Pixel Grid Capture!

The goal of this game is simple: cover the grid! You click pixels as quickly as you can, working with your team, composed of those of the same color family as you.

Your username is your hex code. If you already have one, simply enter it below and click "Existing Username." If not, choose a color family and click "New Username" to receive one and start playing.

Note: clicks are capped at one per 1/10 second
